an exploration in exploration marcus alexander todd

as a computer scientist, i tend to think of life in terms of problems and solutions. what are the issues, what are the tools provided to me, and how can i use those tools to come up with a solution? this semester, in the print/photo workshop, i had the opportunity to use my analytical background to frame my thus far undirected artistic pursuits. though i started out trying to edge away from the computer science label while, at the same time, purposely avoiding the "artist" aesthetic, eventually i did manage to reconcile the two halves of my brain. art became a problem to me, and i tried to come up with a variety of interesting solutions. here's how it turned out.
