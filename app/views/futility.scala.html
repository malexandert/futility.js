futility

If at first you don't succeed, try try again, they say. But what if trying isn't enough? What if you try and try as many times as your body is able, and yet you still don't succeed. What if we're tasked to do the impossible? Then use a computer, they say. But computers are only so fast and clever. Eventually, they reach their limit as well. And yet, they keep trying, and trying, and trying… Futility is a program that will attempt to solve one of the hardest open problems in computer science, the 3-colorability problem. However, by its name, it will never stop. Though computers will get smarter and smarter as the days go on, there are problems where even they can just try and try and try. It’s kind of beautiful to watch.
